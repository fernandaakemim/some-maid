FAQs

Here are some of the most frequent questions we hear from our clients, but don’t hesitate to contact us should you have any additional questions!

  • What services do you offer?

    We offer a variety of cleaning services including residential cleaning, commercial cleaning, Airbnb cleaning, post-construction cleaning, pre-sale cleaning, move-in cleaning, maintenance cleaning, and deep cleaning.

  • What is included in the maintenance cleaning?

    Our maintenance cleaning includes dusting, vacuuming, mopping, cleaning bathrooms, and kitchen surfaces. This service is designed to keep your space looking tidy on a regular basis.

  • What is included in the deep cleaning?

    Our deep cleaning service includes all the elements of our maintenance cleaning, but with extra attention to detail. We will thoroughly clean all surfaces, including baseboards, blinds, and light fixtures. We'll also clean inside and outside of cabinets and drawers, and do a thorough cleaning of the bathrooms and kitchen. This service is designed for homes or businesses that haven't been cleaned in a while, or for those who want a more thorough cleaning.

  • Do you offer extra services, like carpet, oven, and fridge cleaning?

    Yes, we offer additional cleaning services, such as carpet cleaning, oven cleaning, and fridge cleaning. They are included in the deep cleaning package, but please let us know ahead of time if you would like to add any of these services to your regular cleaning at an additional fee.

  • Are your services insured?

    Yes, we carry full liability insurance to give you peace of mind knowing that your property, you, and our staff are protected during our services.

  • What if I don’t like the results of the service provided?

    Your satisfaction is our priority. We offer a 24-hour satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with our cleaning, we'll come back and re-do the work free of charge.

  • Do I need to sign a contract?

    No, we don't require our clients to sign any contracts, even if you hire us on a recurrent basis. You can rely on us to provide top-quality cleaning services without any long-term commitment.

  • Do you bring your own cleaning supplies and equipment?

    Yes, we provide all the necessary cleaning supplies and equipment needed for our services. However, if you have specific products you prefer us to use, our staff will use the products provided by you. Let us know so we can arrange it ahead of time.

  • How do I schedule a cleaning appointment?

    You can easily schedule a cleaning appointment by filling out the contact form on our website or by calling us. We'll work with you to find a time that fits your schedule for a first time visit and estimate.

  • How can I cancel or reschedule my cleaning service?

    To cancel or reschedule your cleaning service, please contact us as soon as possible. We'll work with you to find a time that fits your schedule. Please note that cancelling or rescheduling in less than 48 hours will result in a 50 USD fee. We do this so we can best serve all our clients in the most efficient way and cover part of the opportunity costs incurred on these cases.

  • What areas do you serve?

    We primarily serve the Atlanta metro area, but please contact us to confirm if we can provide services to your specific location.

  • Do you offer any discounts or promotions?

    Yes, we offer occasional promotions and discounts throughout the year. Be sure to check our website or follow us on social media to stay updated on our current offers.

  • Do you support any charities or causes?

    Yes, we support and donate to autism research institutions. When you choose Bossa Nova Cleaning Atlanta, you're not just supporting a cleaning service, but a cause that's close to our hearts.

  • How can I contact Bossa Nova Cleaning Atlanta?

    You can contact us by phone, email, or by filling out the contact form on our website. Our contact information can be found on the Contact page.
