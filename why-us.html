Why Us

At Bossa Nova Cleaning Atlanta, we pride ourselves on providing exceptional cleaning services that exceed our client's expectations. Here are a few reasons why you should choose us.

Insured Services

We carry full liability insurance to give you peace of mind knowing that your property, you, and our staff are protected during our services.

Quality

We have more than 15 years of experience in the cleaning industry. Our team is trained to be detail-oriented and thorough in every cleaning task. We strive to leave no surface untouched, ensuring that your space is spotless and ready for you to enjoy.

24-Hour Warranty

We stand behind the quality of our work and offer a 24-hour satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with our cleaning, we'll come back and re-do the work free of charge.

Time-Saving

We do the hard work for you, freeing up your valuable time. Our team will efficiently clean your space, leaving you with more time to focus on what matters most. Say goodbye to spending your weekends cleaning and let us take care of it for you!

No Contracts

We believe that our work speaks for itself, and we don't require our clients to sign any contracts. You can rely on us to provide top-quality cleaning services without any long-term commitment.

Social Responsibility

We are committed to giving back to our community, which is why we support and donate to Autism research institutions. When you choose Bossa Nova Cleaning Atlanta, you're not just supporting a cleaning service, but a cause that's close to our hearts.
