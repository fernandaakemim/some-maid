Join the Bossa Nova Cleaning Atlanta Team!

We're always looking for talented and dedicated individuals to join our team of cleaning professionals. At Bossa Nova Cleaning Atlanta, we value hard work, attention to detail, and a commitment to exceptional customer service.

If you're looking for a rewarding job in the cleaning industry, we'd love to hear from you! We offer flexible hours, competitive pay, and a supportive work environment.

Our ideal candidates have:

  • A positive attitude and a strong work ethic
  • Attention to detail and commitment to quality work
  • Willingness to learn and evolve
  • Availability to work weekdays and some weekends
  • Previous cleaning experience is not required – we’ll train you!
  • Car and driver’s license are not required
