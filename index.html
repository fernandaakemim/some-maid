Spotless and Reliable

Cleanliness is paramount to a happy and healthy home. We'll take care of that for you!

Welcome to Bossa Nova Cleaning Atlanta, your trusted partner for your cleaning needs. We provide a range of cleaning services to meet your needs and exceed your expectations.

Our services cover 3 main types of properties:

  • Residential
  • Commercial
  • AirBnb

Residential

At Bossa Nova Cleaning Atlanta, we understand that your home is your sanctuary. That's why we offer top-quality residential cleaning services to keep your living space clean and comfortable. Our team of trained professionals uses eco-friendly products and techniques to ensure a spotless and safe home environment for you and your family.

Request a Free Estimate

Commercial

A clean and organized workspace is essential for productivity and employee satisfaction. That's why Bossa Nova Cleaning Atlanta provides reliable and professional commercial cleaning services. We'll work with you to create a customized cleaning plan that meets your specific needs and budget. From office buildings to retail spaces, we'll keep your business looking its best.

Request a Free Estimate

Airbnb

As an AirBnb host, you want to provide your guests with a clean and welcoming space. Bossa Nova Cleaning Atlanta specializes in AirBnb cleaning services, ensuring that your property is ready for your guests' arrival. We'll deep clean your property, replace linens and towels, and restock supplies to make sure your guests have everything they need for a comfortable stay.
