About Us

At Bossa Nova Cleaning Atlanta, we're passionate about providing exceptional cleaning services to our clients. We were founded by Sabrina and Vitor, both Japanese-Brazilian entrepreneurs. Sabrina brought the Japanese-Brazilian culture of cleanliness to her services in Atlanta and has been serving the area with pride since 2006. Vitor has 14 years of experience in management consulting and startups.

Our team is made of dedicated professionals who take pride in their work. We understand that every client has unique needs, which is why we work closely with you to develop a customized cleaning plan that fits your schedule and cleaning preferences. We are detail oriented and use the best equipment and products to leave your home or office sparkling clean and with a peaceful scent.
